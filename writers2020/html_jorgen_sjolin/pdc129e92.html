m1. ?? Stewart (dau of Robert Stewart, 1st Lord Lorn)

There is some confusion regarding the identity of Sir Robert's wife. We show both of the alternatives and point out that he may have married first one then the other. We show that but stress that this is a presumption. The precedence amongst the Erskine records to naming the Stewart daughter before Elizabeth Lindsay suggests (but does not prove) that Robert's heir was born by that Stewart.
