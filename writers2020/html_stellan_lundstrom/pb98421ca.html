Noteringar

Andrew, 3rd Lord Ochiltree, son of Andrew, Master of Ochiltree, who died in his father's lifetime. He ruined himself and resigned his Scotch estates and title - sold barony of Ochiltree to his cousin James of Killeith and was created Baron Castle Stuart of County Tyrone in Ireland (see No.43). He received grants of land in Tyrone Ireland, and was given a Peerage in Ireland 1619 - Lord Castle Stewart (Ireland). He died 1629, leaving tree sons.
