He had 5 illegitimate children.

See the note above about the conflicting reports of the ancestry of the Stewarts of Avandale, etc. We presume that those of the 7 men reported to have been sons of this James in BE1883 (Stewart of Albany) who have not been attributed to his brother Walter are indeed attributable to James (the youngest, James of Baldorran, appears certainly to be so). Matilda, shown by TSP (Albany) and BE1883 as daughter of James, is reported by the Edmonstone records to be Walter's daughter (see above).
